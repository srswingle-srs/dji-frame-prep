## [1.0.0] - 2026-06-05

### Added
- Initial public release.
- DJI `.MP4` + `.SRT` video-to-frame extraction with automatic geotagging.
- WebODM-ready `geo.txt` output.
- Crosshatch and Lawnmower flight-pattern presets.
- Altitude filtering with non-destructive `rejected/` subfolder.
- Preview of estimated frame count and output size.
- Advanced settings: Frame Interval, Altitude Band, Altitude Source, JPEG Quality.
- Per-run `manifest.json` capturing settings and results.
- `run.bat` launcher with automatic dependency installation.
